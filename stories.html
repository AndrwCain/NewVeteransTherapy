Highlight Story: Joshua

Returning from war, the silent battles within me raged. Sleepless nights, vivid nightmares, and an unshakable hyperawareness clung to my every step, an invisible weight I couldn't shed. The once-familiar suburban sounds now triggered echoes of the battlefield. A sudden loud bang shattered my calm, plunging me into panic. My wife, Sarah, a steadfast ally, recognized our journey to healing had just begun. Therapy sessions, art as my outlet, and newfound camaraderie with fellow veterans became lifelines in the struggle. Setbacks were inevitable, but with unwavering support, I pressed on. Sharing my story at a veterans' event marked a turning point, unraveling the stigma.
